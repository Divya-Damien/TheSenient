Identity-Focused Therapy

Identity-Focused Therapy

At various points in our lives we may feel stuck or struggle to move forward from a shift in a relationship, job, or our families, chosen or not. We may also find it difficult to (re)discover ourselves or are challenged and confused by our sexuality, expression, and identity. If this feels familiar to you, you are not alone. My goal in therapy is to walk with you through these transitions and uncertainties of identity and purpose with self-compassion and awareness toward a future filled with confidence regardless of where you currently find yourself.

Hello, I'm Andrea

I am Andrea Rodriguez, a licensed Mental Health Counselor in Florida. I completed a Bachelor of of Science in Psychology and a Master of Science in Psychology with a major in Mental Health Counseling from CAU. Currently, I am a Doctoral student working toward attaining my Ph.D. in Clinical Sexology from IICS. I have been officially counseling individuals since 2019, but if you ask my friends/family they will tell you I have been an unpaid therapist for much longer! I particularly enjoy working with those who struggle with relationship/intimacy issues, attachment difficulties and anxiety.

How I Work

I specialize in working with individuals and couples who struggle with relationship issues, attachment difficulties and anxiety. I utilized evidence-based approaches such as cognitive behavioral therapy, clinical hypnosis and solution-focused strategies.

My approach is curious, empathetic, down-to-earth and direct. I will give you specific strategies to overcome the symptoms you are experiencing now that organically resolve issues of the past so you can move in the direction you want to go in life.
