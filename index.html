Identity-Focused Therapy

Holistic Psychotherapy

At various points in our lives we may feel stuck or struggle to move forward from a shift in a relationship, job, or our families, chosen or not. We may also find it difficult to (re)discover ourselves or are challenged and confused by our sexuality, expression, and identity. If this feels familiar to you, you are not alone. My goal in therapy is to walk with you through these transitions and uncertainties of identity and purpose with self-compassion and awareness toward a future filled with confidence regardless of where you currently find yourself.

How I Work

I utilize evidence-based holistic approaches such as cognitive behavioral therapy, mindfulness, and hypnotherapy. Therapy with me is down to earth and I don't shy away from cursing or using humor to help us communicate more authentically. I focus on addressing mind, body, spirit, and emotions considering the individual as a whole rather than focusing on symptoms.

I am curious, empathetic, and direct. I will give you specific strategies to overcome the issues you are experiencing now that organically resolve past issues. Let's work together so you can move in the direction you want to go in life.
